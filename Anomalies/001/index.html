BaseMaker.zip, also codenamed Anomaly 001, takes the form of a .zip file, and will randomly spread though random computers or the internet.

The first of 2 existing instances of Anomaly 001 took place on 1/2/1998, when it had appeared on an indivual's personal computer. They proceeded to unzip the file. It was already too late. They couldn't save themselves. The local police of the individual's town was dispatched, and the threat ended up being successfully neutralized after the computer was destroyed. The 2nd instance of BaseMaker.zip occured on 12/2/2000, although this time, the individual had deleted the file off of their computer.


Anomaly 001 is divided into 3 phases

  • Phase 1: Arrival
  • Phase 2: Taking over
  • Phase 3: Spread, Invade, Conquer

Phase 1: Arrival


This is where it begins, and this is the only time you can turn back.
