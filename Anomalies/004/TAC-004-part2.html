Experiment 004 -

The SoulScanner





Documentation

The SoulScanner is a machine that has the capability to scan the mind of an individual in order to create a digital replicate of them. It combines both hardware as well as software in order to achieve such task.

The process of how it does so take take upto a maximum of 1 week, or sometimes even longer if there's any technical issues that intervene. Sometimes there is a risk of technical issues becoming lethal, although this hasn't been confirmed, it's just a theorized possibility.

Any other information regarding aspects such as the blueprint, programming, etc, are all kept in other physical documents that have been locked away within one of the many real life located bases of The Flared Web.




Hardware


Hardware is the aspect of the SoulScanner that's responsible for scanning the mind and porting it to digital form.

It's split in multiple sections, each section having a specific purpose for the functioning of the machine.


Hardware section used to transfer data between hardware and software.
