1/15/2004



Agent Hoffman was comissioned into going back into the secret base of the operations.


It was finally ready.


The sub-group in charge of the project had been developing the machine for quite a while. It was only a matter of time before they would reach a breaking point where they had done it,


they would have finally completed the construction.



Hours spend on end, perfecting something powerful.

Very powerful...


The machine itself was created with knowledge unknown to most men. It was a culmination of the most skilled technicians The Flared Web had to offer, they had essentially created a phenomenon that would only be possible in Science Fiction.

It was the SoulScanner, a powerful program that scans the brain of an end user in order to recreate them in a digital form.


Agent Hoffman prepared himself for what was going to come, potentially putting himself in the balance of life and death if the machine wasn't perfected.
