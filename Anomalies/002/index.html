Anomaly 002, also known as DigiCrawlers, are similar in the form of webcrawlers in search engines. DigiCrawlers will make and index pages during a random search that often attempt to mimic a real webpage. The only thing that makes these pages ineffective most of the time, is the way that the metadata of the pages look. They look like gibberish, nothing makes sense when looking at things such as the page title, the page's description, and any other pages that have been included and marked as similar pages to the one that's indexed.

DigiCrawlers may also appear face-to-face with an individual and call out their real name in an attempt to frighten them into clicking onto one of their indexed pages.

Manifestation Threat Level towards the world Status
Other 3/5 Active









Anomaly 002, also known as DigiCrawlers are creatures that manifest themselves similar to webcrawlers in search engines. They will often attempt to index their own pages during a random search that will try to mimic a real webpage. This fails most of the time due to the metadata of the website, such as the title, description, and other pages marked and indexed as similar pages. They all look like gibberish, and this is the only feature of the pages makes them seem off.

These pages are traps created by DigiCrawlers in order to lure anyone curious enough to click on them.



A screenshotted instance of the pages created and indexed by a DigiCrawler.captured at 4/13/2005




DigiCrawlers may also appear face-to-face with an individual and call out their real name in an attempt to frighten them into clicking onto one of their indexed pages.

The image provided has the individual's name censored for privacy.






But what makes the pages dangerous?

The pages draw the individual who is subjected to them into a trance, the DigiCrawler will then (through unknown means) feed off of the individual's brain impulses. This will lead the victim to be dead.







Incident Report
