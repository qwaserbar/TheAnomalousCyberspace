What are Anomalies?


Anomalies are anything that can range from a simple file on a computer to a publicly accessible website. They tend to possess abnormal or special properties that makes them differ from normal versions of them. These properties can have either both positive or negative effects.


Many of these anomalies have been documented and compiled for further researching purposes.
